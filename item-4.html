Café Venetia

Project Café Venetia Website Redesign

Role Web Designer

Tools Sketch, Illustrator

Length 10 weeks

Café Venetia, which means "Café from Venice", established in 2005 in Palo Alto, serving traditional Italian coffee, food and wine with the Slow Food Movement guidance. It aims to bring to customers the authentic Italian coffee and experience in the sense of its food and ambiance.


Café Venetia has a group of loyal customers and they love the environment that the chic café boutique has brought to them. In order to better serve the customers and bring more future customers, Café Venetia would love to redesign its old website.

Assessment

Purpose of the Website:

Primary purpose: Information about the place and about the coffee servings.

Secondary purpose: Online shopping.


Problems of the Current Website:

• The look is old and not appealing.

• Too much information on one page and is not clearly presented.

• The cafe menu is too simple and can't find basic information.

• The call to action is not obviously presented (doesn’t make people want to come and buy things online).

Design Concept/Intent

User Friendly | Modern but Classic | Easy Navigation | Clear Information


Design Objectives:

• Simplify navigation and pagination.

• Simplify information.

• Change font and color.

• Make shopping experience more enjoyable.

• Design style will be closer to traditional but modern look.
