App Description

Citynote is a mobile application that contains information about city events, interesting stores, based on groups of interest and personality matching. It simplifies access to information at any time and from any place the user would be. It specializes in featured stores recommendation, and ease the contact with interesting people, allowing users to stay connected and participate to friends events. No matter if the users are city residents or travelers, they will discover the funniest and most lovely part of the targeted city life and merge into with their own style. Each user will enjoy their time in the city the way he/she feels most comfortable with.
