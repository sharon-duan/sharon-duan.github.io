Café Venetia

Brewing Italian authenticity online

Project Overview

Café Venetia, established in 2005 in Palo Alto, serves traditional Italian coffee, food and wine, wollowing the "Slow Food Movement" guidance. It aims to bring to customers the authentic Italian coffee experience as well as its food and ambiance. The project was to redesign the website, provide information about the place and about the coffee servings, and add an online shopping service.
